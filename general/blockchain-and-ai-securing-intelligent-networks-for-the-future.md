# Blockchain and AI: Securing Intelligent Networks for the Future

The rapid expansion of the [[Internet of Everything]] (IoE) is fundamentally altering the way people, data, and machines interact. By integrating domains such as the [[integrating-artificial-intelligence-physics-and-internet-of-things-a-framework-f|Internet of Things]] (IoT), [[Internet of Vehicles]] (IoV), and the [[Internet of Healthcare]] (IoH), this paradigm enables unprecedented levels of automation and efficiency.
