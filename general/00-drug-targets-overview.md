# [[alzheimers-disease|Alzheimer's Disease Drug Targets (2025-2026 Pipeline)]]

This page serves as a master directory for all emerging **pre-clinical** therapeutic targets for [[targeting-phgdh-for-alzheimers-disease-drug-discovery-strategies|Alzheimer's Disease]] (AD). This repository focuses on novel molecular entities and mechanisms identified through high-throughput screening, CRISPR-cas9 functional genomics, and single-cell transcriptomics as of the 2025-2026 research cycle.

---

## 1. [[amyloid-pathway-targets|Amyloid Pathway]]
*Focus: Reducing the production and aggregation of Aβ peptides.*

*   **[[BACE1]] (Beta-site APP Cleaving Enzyme 1)**
    *   **Pathway:** Amyloidogenic processing of APP.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Next-generation selective allosteric modulators).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Broad Institute]], [[Genentech]].
*   **[[Gamma-Secretase]] Complex**
    *   **Pathway:** Intra-membrane cleavage of APP.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (GSMs - Gamma-secretase modulators to bypass Notch toxicity).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[UC San Diego]], [[Novartis Research]].
*   **Anti-Amyloid Antibodies (Next-Gen)**
    *   **Pathway:** Passive immunization/Amyloid clearance.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Nanobodies and Bispecific antibodies targeting oligomers).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Cambridge]], [[Eli Lilly]].

---

## 2. [[tau-pathway-targets|Tau Pathway]]
*Focus: Prevention of hyperphosphorylation and neurofibrillary tangle (NFT) formation.*

*   **[[GSK3b]] (Glycogen Synthase Kinase 3 Beta)**
    *   **Pathway:** Tau hyperphosphorylation kinase.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Small molecule inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Mount Sinai]], [[Washington University in St. Louis]].
*   **[[CDK5]] (Cyclin-Dependent Kinase 5)**
    *   **Pathway:** Tau-associated kinase activity.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Selective inhibition).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Harvard Medical School]].
*   **[[Tau Immunotherapy]]**
    *   **Pathway:** Clearance of extracellular Tau seeds.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Intracellular-targeted antibodies).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[UCSF]], [[University of Minnesota]].
*   **[[O-GlcNAcase]] (OGA)**
    *   **Pathway:** Regulation of Tau O-GlcNAcylation (competing with phosphorylation).
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (OGA inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Mass General Brigham]].

---

3. [[neuroinflammation-targets|Neuroinflammation]]
*Focus: Modulating microglial activation and the innate immune response.*

*   **[[TREM2]] (Triggering Receptor Expressed on Myeloid cells 2)**
    *   **Pathway:** Microglial survival and phagocytosis.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Agonistic antibodies/small molecules).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[UCSF]], [[Sangamo Therapeutics]].
*   **[[CD33]]**
    *   **Pathway:** Inhibition of microglial clearance of Aβ.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Antagonists/Blockers).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Pennsylvania]], [[Regeneron]].
*   **[[NLRP3]] (Inflammasome)**
    *   **Pathway:** Activation of IL-1β and neuroinflammation.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Small molecule inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Max Planck Institute]], [[Scripps Research]].
*   **[[bridging-mri-and-pet-physiology-untangling-complementarity-through-orthogonal-re|Complement]] System (C1q, C3)**
    *   **Pathway:** Synaptic pruning and neurotoxicity.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Complement inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Zurich]], [[Rockefeller University]].

---

## 4. [[Synaptic Plasticity]]
*Focus: Maintaining cognitive function and preventing synaptic loss.*

*   **[[PDE Inhibitors]] (Phosphodiesterase)**
    *   **Pathway:** Cyclic nucleotide (cAMP/cGMP) regulation.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (PDE4/PDE10 selective).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Oxford]], [[GlaxoSmithKline]].
*   **[[mGluR5]] (Metabotropic Glutamate Receptor 5)**
    *   **Pathway:** Long-term potentiation (LTP) modulation.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Positive allosteric modulators).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA)]].
*   **[[NMDA]] Receptor Modulators**
    *   **Pathway:** Glutamate-induced excitotoxicity.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Glycine-site antagonists).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Mayo Clinic]].
*   **[[5-HT6]] (Serotonin Receptor)**
    *   **Pathway:** Neuromodulation of cholinergic systems.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Antagonists).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Edinburgh]].

---

## 5. [[metabolic-dysregulation-in-alzheimers-disease|Metabolic Dysregulation]]
*Focus: Addressing "Type 3 Diabetes" and bioenergetic failure.*

*   **[[temporally-phenotyping-glp-1ra-case-reports-with-large-language-models-a-textual|GLP-1R]] (Glucagon-like Peptide-1 Receptor)**
    *   **Pathway:** Insulin signaling and neuroprotection.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical/Early Clinical Translation.
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Novo Nordisk]], [[Eli Lilly]].
*   **[[AMPK]] (AMP-activated Protein Kinase)**
    *   **Pathway:** Cellular energy homeostasis and autophagy.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (AMPK activators).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[UPenn]].
*   **[[mTOR]] (Mammalian Target of Rapamycin)**
    *   **Pathway:** Protein synthesis and autophagy regulation.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (mTORC1 inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Albert Einstein College of Medicine]].
*   **[[mitochondrial-dysfunction|Mitochondrial]] Complex I/IV**
    *   **Pathway:** Oxidative phosphorylation and ROS reduction.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Mitochondrial-targeted antioxidants).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[the-limits-of-bio-molecular-modeling-with-large-language-models-a-cross-scale-ev|MIT]].

---

## 6. [[epigenetic-regulation|Epigenetic Regulation]]
*Focus: Restoring gene expression patterns essential for memory.*

*   **[[HDAC]] (Histone Deacetylase)**
    *   **Pathway:** Chromatin remodeling and synaptic gene expression.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Class I HDAC inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Toronto]].
*   **[[between-century-and-poet-graph-based-lexical-semantic-change-in-persian-poetry|BET]] (Bromodomain and Extra-Terminal domain)**
    *   **Pathway:** Reader proteins in inflammatory signaling.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (BET inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Dana-Farber Cancer Institute]].
*   **[[DNA Methyltransferases]] (DNMT)**
    *   **Pathway:** DNA methylation and gene silencing.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical.
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of California, Berkeley]].

---

## 7. [[Proteostasis]]
*Focus: Enhancing the clearance of misfolded protein aggregates.*

*   **[[autophagy|Autophagy]] (LC3/Atg proteins)**
    *   **Pathway:** Lysosomal degradation of aggregates.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Autophagy enhancers).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Janelia Research Campus]].
*   **[[ubiquitin-proteasome-system|Proteasome]] (Ubiquitin-Proteasome System)**
    *   **Pathway:** Degradation of soluble toxic proteins.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Proteasome activators).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Cambridge]].
*   **[[UPR]] (Unfolded Protein Response)**
    *   **Pathway:** ER stress management (PERK, IRE1α).
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (ER stress alleviators).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Southern California]].

---

## 8. [[Vascular Integrity]]
*Focus: Protecting the Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB) and cerebrovascular health.*

*   **[[adversarially-and-distributionally-robust-virtual-energy-storage-systems-via-the|RAGE]] (Receptor for Advanced Glycation End-products)**
    *   **Pathway:** Pro-inflammatory signaling and BBB breakdown.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (RAGE antagonists).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Maryland]].
*   **[[LRP1]] (Low-density Lipoprotein Receptor-related Protein 1)**
    *   **Pathway:** Aβ transport across the BBB.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (LRP1 agonists).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[University of Washington]].

---

## 9. [[Novel Frontiers]]
*Focus: Cutting-edge biological paradigms (2025+).*

*   **[[apoe4-lipid-targets|APOE4]] Modulation**
    *   **Pathway:** Genetic risk factor; lipid metabolism.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Gene editing/CRISPR, Small molecule converters).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Broad Institute]], [[Regeneron]].
*   **[[Ferroptosis]]**
    *   **Pathway:** Iron-dependent lipid peroxidation.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Ferroptosis inhibitors).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[UT Southwestern]].
*   **[[Senolytics]]**
    *   **Pathway:** Clearing of senescent (zombie) cells in the CNS.
    *   **Development Stage:** Pre-clinical (Senolytic cocktails).
    *   **Key Labs:** [[Mayo Clinic]], [[Buck Institute]].
