# Predicting Activity Cliffs for Autonomous Medicinal Chemistry

The study "Predicting Activity Cliffs for Autonomous Medicinal Chemistry" (arXiv:2604.07560) addresses a fundamental bottleneck in [[computational chemistry]]: the identification of [[predicting-activity-cliffs-for-autonomous-medicinal-chemistry|activity cliffs]]. These are specific instances where minute structural alterations to a molecule result in disproportionately large shifts in biological potency.
