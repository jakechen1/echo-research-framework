# Turing Test on Screen: A Benchmark for Mobile GUI Agent Humanization

The rapid advancement of autonomous [[GUI Agents]] has introduced a novel conflict within digital ecosystems: the tension between automation and the rising implementation of anti-detection countermeasures by mobile platforms. While much of the current research in [[Artificial Intelligence]] focuses on the utility and robustness of these agents, this paper argues that for agents to survive in human-centric environments, they must develop **Humanization** capabilities.
