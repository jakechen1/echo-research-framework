In modern, large-scale supermarket operations, managing the lifecycle of goods—from demand forecasting and procurement to supplier coordination and inventory replenishment—relies on high-volume, manual, and often fragmented workflows. These processes are notoriously decision-intensive and increasingly difficult to scale without an exponential increase in human labor and operational costs.
