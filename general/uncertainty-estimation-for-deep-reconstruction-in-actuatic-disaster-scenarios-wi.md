# Uncertainty Estimation for Deep Reconstruction in Aquatic Disaster Scenarios with Autonomous Vehicles

## Overview
In the context of [[Autonomous Vehicles]] operating in aquatic disaster environments, the ability to accurately reconstruct environmental scalar fields from sparse, onboard sensor observations is a critical capability. This paper investigates the necessity of [[improving-semantic-uncertainty-quantification-in-language-model-question-answeri|Uncertainty Quantification]] (UQ) to supplement traditional point estimates.
