# SUMMIR: A Hallucination-Aware Framework for Ranking Sports Insights from LLMs

As digital sports journalism expands, the ability to automatically extract relevant pre-game and post-game insights from massive volumes of text is vital for enhancing user engagement and comprehension. The **SUMMIR** framework addresses the challenge of distilling meaningful information from sports news while maintaining high factual reliability and personal relevance.
