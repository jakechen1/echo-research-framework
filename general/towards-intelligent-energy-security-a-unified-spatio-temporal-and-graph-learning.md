# Towards Intelligent Energy Security

The research paper "Towards Intelligent Energy Security" introduces the **SmartGuard Energy Intelligence System (SGEIS)**, a sophisticated [[artificial-intelligence-and-the-structure-of-mathematics|Artificial Intelligence]] framework designed to address the growing problem of electricity theft and non-technical losses (NTLs) within modern [[Smart Grid]] infrastructures. As power grids become more digitized, detecting unauthorized consumption is essential for maintaining economic stability and grid reliability.
