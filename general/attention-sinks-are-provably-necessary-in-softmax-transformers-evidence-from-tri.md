# Attention Sinks Are Provably Necessary in Softmax Transformers

The paper **"Attention Sinks Are Provably Necessary in Softmax Transformers: Evidence from Trigger-Conditional Tasks"** investigates the functional role of "attention sinks"—the phenomenon where attention probability mass concentrates on a fixed, content-agnostic position within a [[crft-consistent-recurrent-feature-flow-transformer-for-cross-modal-image-registr|Transformer]] model. While these sinks were previously often viewed as an incidental byproduct of the optimization or training process, this research provides mathematical evidence that they are a functional necessity within [[attention-sinks-are-provably-necessary-in-softmax-transformers-evidence-from-tri|Softmax]]-based attention mechanisms.
