# Help Without Being Asked: A Deployed Proactive Agent System for On-Call Support with Continuous Self-Improvement

The management of large-scale [[Cloud Computing]] platforms involves handling thousands of customer tickets daily. Traditionally, this is managed through reactive processes where [[Large Language Models]] act as the first line of defense. However, a significant bottleneck occurs when issues are escalated to human analysts: existing reactive agents typically disengage, losing the ability to assist in the follow-up phase or learn from the eventual human-led resolution.

## The Vigil System

This paper introduces **Vigil**, a novel proactive agent system designed to bridge the gap in the on-call lifecycle.
