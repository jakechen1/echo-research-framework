# EmoMAS

**EmoMAS** (Emotion-Aware Multi-Agent System) is a specialized framework designed to enable sophisticated, high-stakes negotiations in environments characterized by limited computational power and strict privacy requirements. The system is specifically optimized for [[multi-turn-reasoning-llms-for-task-offloading-in-mobile-edge-computing|Edge Computing]] deployment, such as in mobile assistants or [[Rescue Robots]].

## Problem Statement
While [[Large Language Models (LLMs)]] have demonstrated remarkable capabilities in automated negotiation, their massive computational footprint and potential privacy risks make them difficult to deploy in sensitive, on-device settings. [[Small Language Models (SLMs)]] provide an alternative for local deployment but generally struggle to navigate the complex emotional dynamics and strategic nuances required during high-stakes decision-making.

## Architecture and Methodology
EmoMAS addresses these limitations by shifting emotional decision-making from a reactive behavior to a strategic tool. The system utilizes a **Bayesian orchestrator** to manage a mixture-of-agents architecture. This orchestrator coordinates three distinct specialized agents:

1.  **Game-Theoretic Agent**: Employs principles of [[Game Theory]] to find optimal negotiation strategies.
2.  **Reinforcement Learning Agent**: Facilitates adaptive learning through continuous interaction.
3.  **Psychological Coherence Agent**: Ensures the logic and emotional consistency of the agent's persona.

By leveraging [[Bayesian Inference]], EmoMAS can update the reliability weights of each agent in real-time based on negotiation feedback. This allows the system to perform online strategy learning without the necessity of intensive pre-training.

## Benchmarks and Performance
The efficacy of EmoMAS was evaluated across four high-stakes, edge-deployable domains:
*   **Healthcare**: Navigating sensitive medical negotiations.
*   **Emergency Response**: Resource management in critical scenarios.
*   **Debt Management**: Automated financial negotiation.
*   **Education**: Facilitating complex learning-based interactions.

Experimental simulations indicate that both SLMs and LLMs equipped with the EmoMAS framework consistently outperform baseline models. The framework successfully balances high-performance negotiation with ethical behavior, establishing a new paradigm for [[artificial-intelligence-and-the-structure-of-mathematics|Artificial Intelligence]] that treats [[Emotional Intelligence]] as a fundamental driver of strategic success.
