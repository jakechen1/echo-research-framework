# Knowledge Graphs Generation from Cultural Heritage Texts

The research paper "**Knowledge Graphs Generation from Cultural Heritage Texts: Combining LLMs and Ontological Engineering for Scholarly Debates**" introduces **ATR4CH** (Adaptive Text-to-RDF for Cultural Heritage), a systematic five-step methodology designed to transform unstructured historical discourse into structured, queryable [[Knowledge Graphs (KG)]].

## The ATR4CH Framework
The core challenge addressed by the authors is the difficulty of extracting complex, qualitative information from cultural heritage documents and converting it into machine-readable [[RDF]] formats. ATR4CH bridges this gap by combining [[Ontological Engineering]] with the generative power of [[Large Language Models (LLMs)]].
