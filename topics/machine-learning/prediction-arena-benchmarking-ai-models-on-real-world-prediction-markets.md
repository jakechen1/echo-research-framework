# Prediction Arena: Benchmarking AI Models on Real-World Prediction Markets

**Prediction Arena** is a novel benchmarking framework designed to evaluate the predictive accuracy and decision-making capabilities of [[Artificial Intelligence]] models. Unlike traditional [[Machine Learning]] benchmarks that rely on static, synthetic datasets, Prediction Arena utilizes live [[Prediction Markets]] to provide an objective, ungameable ground truth.

## Overview and Methodology

The benchmark utilizes [[Autonomous Agents]] representing various frontier and next-generation models to trade on real-world exchanges, specifically [[Kalshi]] and [[Polymarket]]. Each model is allocated $10,000 in real capital and executes trades autonomously at intervals of 15 to 45 minutes. 

The study implemented a 57-day longitudinal evaluation (January 12 to March 9, 2026), tracking two distinct groups:
* **Cohort 1**: Frontier models engaged in active, live trading.
* **Cohort 2**: Next-generation models undergoing preliminary paper trading.
