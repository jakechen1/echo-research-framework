# Banana100: Breaking NR-IQA Metrics by 100 Iterative Image Replications with Nano Banana Pro

The research paper "Banana100: Breaking NR-IQA Metrics by 100 Iterative Image Replications with Nano Banana Pro" addresses a fundamental vulnerability in the lifecycle of [[multi-modal-agentic-systems|Multi-modal Agentic Systems]]. While contemporary [[image-editing-models|Image Editing Models]] demonstrate remarkable success in single-turn instruction following, this study identifies a critical weakness in multi-turn workflows: the phenomenon of iterative degradation.
