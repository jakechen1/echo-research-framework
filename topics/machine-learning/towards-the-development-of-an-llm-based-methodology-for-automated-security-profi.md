# Towards the Development of an LLM-Based Methodology for Automated Security Profiling in Compliance with Ukrainian Cybersecurity Regulations

This article explores the intersection of [[artificial-intelligence-and-the-structure-of-mathematics|Artificial Intelligence]] and [[cybersecurity]] management, specifically focusing on the modernization of regulatory compliance within the Ukrainian digital landscape. As the pace of information technology development accelerates, cybersecurity specialists face increasing pressure to refine processes to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive information.

### Regulatory Context and Frameworks
The research uses Ukraine as a primary case study to examine how international best practices are integrated into national regulatory systems. Specifically, it analyzes the implementation of the [[isoiec-27001|ISO/IEC 27001]] standard and the [[nist-cybersecurity-framework|NIST Cybersecurity Framework]] within Ukrainian normative documents. A significant theme in this evolution is the paradigm shift from traditional, static compliance models to more adaptive, [[risk-based-approaches|risk-based approaches]]. This transition is essential for maintaining security integrity in environments characterized by high-intensity hybrid threats.

### Proposed LLM-Based Methodology
To mitigate the complexities of manual compliance, the paper introduces a methodology for automating the development of target security profiles. This approach leverages [[large-language-models-for-outpatient-referral-problem-definition-benchmarking-an|Large Language Models]] (LLMs) enhanced by [[contradictions-in-context-challenges-for-retrieval-augmented-generation-in-healt|Retrieval-Augmented Generation]] (RAG). 

The core components of the proposed system include:
* **RAG-Based Advisor:** A specialized tool designed to navigate complex regulatory landscapes.
* **Vector Database:** The integration of a [[vector-database|vector database]] containing both national regulations and specific organizational policies.
* **Automated Profiling:** A workflow that reduces manual complexity and minimizes the potential for human error.

### Conclusion
By utilizing [[a-comparative-analysis-of-machine-learning-models-in-shap-analysis|machine-learning]] techniques, the proposed methodology ensures that technical controls are strictly aligned with legal and regulatory requirements. This study provides a structured, automated workflow that serves as a critical contribution to the field of AI-assisted security management, providing a scalable solution for organizations operating under rigorous regulatory scrutiny.
