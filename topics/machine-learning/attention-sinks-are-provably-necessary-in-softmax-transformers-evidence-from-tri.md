# Attention Sinks Are Provably Necessary in Softmax Transformers

The paper **"Attention Sinks Are Provably Necessary in Softmax Transformers: Evidence from Trigger-Conditional Tasks"** investigates the functional role of "attention sinks"—the phenomenon where attention probability mass concentrates on a fixed, content-agnostic position within a [[Transformer]] model. While these sinks were previously often viewed as an incidental byproduct of the optimization or training process, this research provides mathematical evidence that they are a functional necessity within [[Softmax]]-based attention mechanisms.
