# Can LLMs Beat Classical Hyperparmeter Optimization Algorithms? A Study on autoresearch

The research paper "Can LLMs Beat Classical Hyperparameter Optimization Algorithms? A Study on autoresearch" investigates the potential of [[large-language-models-for-outpatient-referral-problem-definition-benchmarking-an|Large Language Models]] (LLMs) to replace or augment traditional [[can-llms-beat-classical-hyperparameter-optimization-algorithms-a-study-on-autore|Hyperparameter Optimization]] (HPO) algorithms. The study centers on **autoresearch**, a framework that enables an LLM agent to navigate unconstrained search spaces by directly editing training source code.
