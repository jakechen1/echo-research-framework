# An Automated Survey of Generative Artificial Intelligence

This paper provides a comprehensive and automated overview of the rapidly evolving landscape of [[an-automated-survey-of-generative-artificial-intelligence-large-language-models-|Generative Artificial Intelligence]] as of early 2026. The survey serves as a longitudinal study of the transition from early [[large-language-models-for-outpatient-referral-problem-definition-benchmarking-an|Large Language Models]] (LLMs) to the frontier architectures that define the current era of [[a-comparative-analysis-of-machine-learning-models-in-shap-analysis|Machine Learning]].

## Model Analysis and Architectures

A central contribution of this work is the detailed comparative analysis of both open-weight and proprietary model families. The authors evaluate the technical innovations and training regimes of several prominent systems, including:

* **Open-Weight Systems:** Significant focus is placed on the [[deepseek|DeepSeek]] lineage (including DeepSeek-V3, R1, and the anticipated V4), the [[qwen|Qwen]] series, [[llama-4|LLaMA 4]], [[mistral-large-3|Mistral Large 3]], and smaller efficient models like [[gemma-3|Gemma 3]] and [[phi-4|Phi-4]].
* **Proprietary Systems:** The survey assesses the performance of industry leaders such as [[gpt-54|GPT-5.4]], [[gemini-31-pro|Gemini 3.1 Pro]], [[claude-opus-46|Claude Opus 4.6]], and [[grok-420|Grok 4.20]].

Performance metrics are derived from empirical benchmarks and real-world user preference data from the [[chatbot-arena|Chatbot Arena]] leaderboard.

## Deployment Protocols and Agentic Ecosystems

The survey moves beyond static model evaluation to examine the protocols that facilitate [[undetectable-conversations-between-ai-agents-via-pseudorandom-noise-resilient-ke|AI Agents]] and interoperability. Key research areas discussed include:

* [[retrieval-augmented-generation-rag|Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)]] for enhancing context accuracy.
* The [[model-context-protocol-mcp|Model Context Protocol (MCP)]] for standardized data interface.
* [[agent-to-agent-protocols|Agent-to-Agent protocols]] and standardized [[function-calling|function calling]] frameworks.
* Modern [[serving-frameworks|serving frameworks]] for scalable deployment.

## Real-World Applications

The research demonstrates the impact of generative technology across fifteen distinct industry sectors. Case studies highlight the integration of AI in [[financial-services|Financial Services]], [[legal-technology|Legal Technology]], [[agriculture|Agriculture]], and [[tourism|Tourism]], showcasing the shift from experimental use to core operational infrastructure.
