# NativQA Framework

The **NativQA Framework** is an advanced system designed to mitigate the pervasive issues of cultural bias, linguistic inequity, and performance gaps observed in modern [[Large Language Models]] (LLMs) and [[Vision-Language Models]] (VLMs). As AI development scales, a significant disparity has emerged between high-resource languages and the diverse, underrepresented regions of the world. NativQA addresses this by enabling the scalable construction of datasets grounded in native, local, and everyday knowledge.

## Framework Overview

The core innovation of the NativQA framework lies in its transition toward [[multimodality]]. While previous iterations of the framework focused on text-based data, this expanded version integrates support for images, audio, and video. This allows the framework to capture a multidimensional view of cultural contexts that text alone cannot convey.

The framework operates through a systematic process:
1. **Seed Queries**: Users define specific queries related to local contexts.
2. **Automated Collection**: The framework utilizes search engines to retrieve location-specific, everyday information.
3. **Dataset Construction**: It transforms the retrieved data into structured, culturally aligned Question-Answering (QA) pairs.

## Scale and Scope

The empirical evaluation of the NativQA framework demonstrates its massive potential for [[data collection]] and model evaluation. The researchers successfully documented:
* **Geographic Diversity**: 39 distinct locations across 24 countries.
* **Linguistic Range**: 7 different languages, spanning from high-resource to extremely low-resource settings.
* **Dataset Magnitude**: Over 300,000 text QA pairs, 312,000 images, and 29,000 videos with accompanying audio.

## Applications in AI

The resources generated by NativQA are intended for two primary uses in the [[machine-learning]] community:
* **[[Benchmarking]]**: Providing a standard for testing how well models handle cultural nuances and local knowledge.
* **[[Fine-tuning]]**: Offering high-quality, localized datasets to train models to be more culturally intelligent and less biased.

By bridging the gap between global AI capabilities and local cultural realities, NativQA serves as a foundational tool for developing more equitable and representative [[artificial intelligence]] systems.
