# Simulating the Evolution of Alignment and Values in Machine Intelligence

Current approaches to [[model-alignment|model alignment]] are largely criticized for being applied in a vacuum, focusing primarily on performance within standardized, static benchmarks. The study "Simulating the Evolution of Alignment and Values in Machine Intelligence" (arXiv:2604.05274) proposes a paradigm shift toward longitudinal analysis, examining how alignment methodologies impact populations of models over time.
