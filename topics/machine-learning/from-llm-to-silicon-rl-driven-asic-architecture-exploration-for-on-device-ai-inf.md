# From LLM to Silicon: RL-Driven ASIC Architecture Exploration for On-Device AI Inference

## Overview
The paper presents a novel [[Reinforcement Learning]] (RL)-driven compiler designed to automate the complex design space exploration required for [[ASIC]] (Application-Specific Integrated Circuit) development. As [[Large Language Models]] (LLMs) and vision models move toward [[Edge AI]] deployment, the need for hardware that is custom-tuned to specific [[Process Nodes]]—ranging from 3nm to 28nm—has become critical.
