# Temporally Phenotyping GLP-1RA Case Reports with Large Language Models

## Overview
The research paper, "Temporally Phenotyping GLP-1RA Case Reports with Large Language Models," addresses a significant challenge in [[clinical-nlp|clinical NLP]]: the extraction of structured, longitudinal data from unstructured medical narratives. Although [[pubmed|PubMed]] case reports provide detailed clinical insights, the timelines within these reports are often expressed in natural language, making them difficult to utilize for automated [[longitudinal-modeling|longitudinal modeling]] or large-scale statistical analysis.
