# A Quasi-Regression Method for the Mediation Analysis of Zero-Inflated Single-Cell Data

The research paper "A Quasi-Regression Method for the Mediation Analysis of Zero-Inflated Single-Cell Data" introduces **QuasiMed**, a specialized statistical framework designed to perform [[causal mediation analysis]] on [[single-cell sequencing]] datasets.

### Context and Problem Statement
Recent advancements in [[single-cell technologies]] have revolutionized our understanding of [[gene regulation]] and cellular heterogeneity
