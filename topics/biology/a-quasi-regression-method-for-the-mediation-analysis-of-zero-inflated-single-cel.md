# A Quasi-Regression Method for the Mediation Analysis of Zero-Inflated Single-Cell Data

The research paper "A Quasi-Regression Method for the Mediation Analysis of Zero-Inflated Single-Cell Data" introduces **QuasiMed**, a specialized statistical framework designed to perform [[causal-mediation-analysis|causal mediation analysis]] on [[single-cell-sequencing|single-cell sequencing]] datasets.
