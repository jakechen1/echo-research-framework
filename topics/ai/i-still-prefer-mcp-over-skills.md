# I still prefer MCP over skills

In the rapidly advancing field of [[Artificial Intelligence]], a fundamental architectural debate has emerged: should AI agents rely on bespoke "skills," or should they utilize a standardized protocol like the [[Model Context Protocol]] (MCP)?

The article "I still prefer MCP over skills" posits that the industry is at a crossroads between fragmented, custom-integrated toolsets and a unified, interoperable ecosystem.
