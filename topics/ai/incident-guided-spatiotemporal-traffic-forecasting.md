# Incident-Guided Spatiotemporal Traffic Forecasting

The research paper "Incident-Guided Spatiotemporal Traffic Forecasting" addresses a critical limitation in modern [[Intelligent Transportation Systems]] (ITS). While recent advancements in [[Deep Learning]] have led to highly effective [[Graph Neural Networks]] (GNNs) for predicting traffic patterns, most existing methods rely exclusively on historical data. This approach overlooks significant external disturbances, such as traffic accidents and adverse weather, which act as sudden, unpredictable shocks to the system.
