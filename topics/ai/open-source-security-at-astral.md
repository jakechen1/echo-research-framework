# Open source security at Astral

The article "Open source security at Astral" explores the methodologies implemented by the creators of high-performance Python tools, such as [[Ruff]] and [[uv]], to maintain the integrity of the [[Open Source]] ecosystem. As Astral's tools have seen rapid adoption across the industry, the importance of securing the [[Software Supply Chain]] has become a central pillar of their development philosophy.

### Navigating the Supply Chain Risk
In modern [[Software Development]], the reliance on deep dependency trees creates significant surface area for potential attacks. Astral addresses the growing threat of supply chain compromises by focusing on how high-profile projects can remain resilient. The core challenge lies in balancing the rapid, collaborative nature of open-source contributions with the rigorous scrutiny required to prevent malicious code injection.
