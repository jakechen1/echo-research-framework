# Modelling Cascading Physical Climate Risk in Supply Chains with Adaptive Firms

This paper introduces a novel, open-source [[Python]] framework designed to simulate the propagation of physical [[Climate Risk]] through complex, spatially-distributed [[Supply Chain]] networks. By integrating [[Geospatial Flood Hazards]] with an [[Agent-Based Modeling]] (ABM) approach, the research captures the intricate relationship between environmental disasters and the resulting economic disruptions affecting both firms and households.
