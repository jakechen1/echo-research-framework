# Mixed-Initiative Context: Structuring and Managing Context for Human-AI Collaboration

In the domain of [[Human-AI Collaboration]], the management of interaction history presents a significant technical challenge. Current paradigms typically treat the context formed during multi-turn interactions as a "flattened" chronological sequence. In this model, the context is treated as a fixed, monolithic entity, lacking any mechanism for dynamic organization or hierarchical management throughout a complex workflow.
