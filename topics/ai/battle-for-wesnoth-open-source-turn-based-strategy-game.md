# Battle for Wesnoth: open-source, turn-based strategy game

*Battle for Wesnoth* is a highly acclaimed [[battle-for-wesnoth-open-source-turn-based-strategy-game|Open-source]] turn-based strategy game set within a rich, expansive fantasy world. As a prominent fixture in the [[indie-games|Indie games]] landscape, the project has maintained a resilient and dedicated following for decades, largely driven by its commitment to free, community-led development and the principle of accessible gaming for all.

The core gameplay mechanics are rooted in classic [[battle-for-wesnoth-open-source-turn-based-strategy-game|Turn-based strategy]] (TBS) principles, utilizing a hex-based movement system where positioning and terrain are critical. Players command diverse factions, navigating through intricate campaigns that involve managing unit types, terrain advantages, and tactical positioning to overcome formidable opponents. The depth of the experience is significantly enhanced by the diverse range of units, each possessing unique strengths and weaknesses, making every combat encounter a complex strategic puzzle.

Crucially, *Battle for Wesnoth* stands as a significant achievement in [[open-source-software|Open-source software]]-driven [[game-development|Game development]]. Unlike commercially driven titles, the game's evolution is shaped by a global community of contributors who handle everything from digital art and music to complex coding updates and engine optimization. This collaborative model allows for a high degree of customization via mods and user-generated content, which has been a primary driver of the game's longevity and content variety.
