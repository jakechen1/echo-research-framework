# Full State-Space Visualisation of the 8-Puzzle

The research paper *"Full State-Space Visualisation of the 8-Puzzle: Feasibility, Design, and Educational Use"* addresses a fundamental challenge in [[Artificial Intelligence]] (AI) pedagogy: the cognitive difficulty learners face when attempting to form accurate mental models of large, complex [[State Space]] structures.

## The Challenge of Scale
[[Search Algorithms]] are foundational topics in AI curricula; however, even classically simple domains can generate massive state spaces. In the case of the 8-puzzle, there are 181,440 reachable states. For students, visualizing the global structure and the specific paths taken by different algorithms through such a vast network of possibilities is nearly impossible through abstract mathematical notation alone.
