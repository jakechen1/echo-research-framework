# Agora: Teaching the Skill of Consensus-Finding with AI Personas Grounded in Human Voice

**Agora** is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to cultivate [[civic-competence|Civic Competence]]—the essential capacity to navigate disagreement, weigh competing values, and arrive at collective decisions. Grounded in the principles of [[deliberative-democracy|Deliberative Democracy]], the platform addresses the inherent difficulty in providing large-scale opportunities for citizens to practice the skills required for democratic governance.

## Core Technology and Methodology

The platform utilizes [[large-language-models-for-outpatient-referral-problem-definition-benchmarking-an|Large Language Models]] (LLMs) to simulate a diverse range of "AI Personas." These personas are uniquely grounded in authentic human voices and perspectives regarding specific policy issues. This ensures that the simulation remains tethered to real-world complexities rather than arbitrary algorithmic outputs.

In the Agora interface, users engage in a continuous cycle of policy development and evaluation:
* **Policy Prototyping:** Users propose and revise specific policy recommendations.
* **Simulated Debate:** Users encounter both supporting and opposing perspectives through AI-driven interactions.
* **Impact Analysis:** Users receive feedback on how specific policy changes affect predicted levels of public support.

A critical differentiator of the Agora platform is the use of **voice explanations**. While traditional analytical tools often rely solely on aggregate support distributions (such as simple data visualizations), Agora provides qualitative, voice-based explanations. These explanations detail the nuanced reasons why a change in policy might increase or decrease support among different personae.

## Research and Findings

In a preliminary study involving 44 university students, researchers evaluated the educational impact of the platform. The findings demonstrated that access to the full interface—specifically the qualitative voice explanations—significantly improved two key areas of development:
1. **[[perspective-taking|Perspective-Taking]]:** The ability to intellectually and empathetically adopt the viewpoints of others.
2. **Nuanced Reasoning:** An increased tendency to formulate statements that acknowledge multiple, often competing, viewpoints.

By providing a scalable, interactive environment for practicing deliberation, Agora represents a promising new direction for the future of [[civic-education|Civic Education]] and the reinforcement of democratic institutions.
